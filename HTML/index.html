Welcome

Welcome to A3 Training and Consultancy! We are a training and consultancy agency that hopes to promote the appreciation and application of Artificial Intelligence (A.I.) in everyday lives. We aim to dissolve the myths surrounding A.I. and reveal the true potential behind its mysterious nature.

What exactly is A.I.?

A.I. is simply a tool that employs Mathematical equations and functions to predict certain outcomes or results with precision. A.I. is not magical; A.I. is not dangerous; A.I. is not smarter than humans. It is a tool and, like all other tools, has the potential to be used for good as well as for bad.

We hope that, through our website, we would be able to expose more people to A.I. and its unexplored potential.

We feel that A.I. is often misunderstood and misrepresented, through its symbolism in novels and movies. We wish to bust the myths surrounding A.I. and, ultimately, unveil its potential to everyone. We welcome anyone and everyone to join us in our endeavour. Be part of the A.I. Revolution today!
Mr Johnny Oswaldo, Founder and Director of A3 Training & Consultancy

Our Humble Beginnings

A3 Training and Consultancy was founded in 1980 by Mr Johnny Oswaldo. He is 73 years old to date and our Company's current beloved Director. Mr Oswaldo studied Engineering during his adolescent years and graduated from MIT with a Master's Degree in Computer Science. Since then, he has endeavoured to enlighten the world about A.I..

Our Initial Challenges

When A3 was first establised, only a handful of people had the slightest inklink of what A.I. was about. Many were adverse to the concept of robots outsmarting humans, therefore we faced numerous obstacles in our endeavour to empower others' lives by A.I..

Our Success

Since the early 21st Century, due to the rapid advancement in Technology globally, we were able to gain some publicity. Finally, we were one step closer to fulfilling our Mission. We have come a long way since 1960, with more than 100, 000 members in the US alone and many more in other parts of the world.

Our Wishes

We love A.I. and we want you to love It the same way we do. Don't ever let anyone get you down! Anyone can code. Happy Coding!
